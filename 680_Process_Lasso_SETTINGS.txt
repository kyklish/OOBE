DisallowedProcesses=C:\Windows\SystemApps\Microsoft.Windows.StartMenuExperienceHost_*\*.exe,C:\Windows\SystemApps\MicrosoftWindows.Client.CBS_*\*.exe,C:\Windows\SystemApps\ShellExperienceHost_*\*.exe

Or you can use pcocesses' names:
DisallowedProcesses=crossdeviceresume.exe,shellexperiencehost.exe,searchhost.exe,startmenuexperiencehost.exe,textinputhost.exe
